Help & Support

How do I report an issue?

Navigate to the "Issue Report" section from the top menu. Fill in all the required details including title, description, and location. You can also upload a photo or video. After filling the form, you can preview your report before final submission.

How can I check the status of my report?

You can view the status of all your submitted issues in the "Status" section. The status will be updated in real-time as our team works on it.

What do the different statuses mean?

  • Pending: Your report has been successfully submitted and is awaiting review by an administrator.
  • In Progress: An administrator has reviewed your report and assigned it for resolution.
  • Resolved: The issue has been addressed and resolved.

Who can I contact for more help?

If you need further assistance, please email our support team at support@portal.com.
